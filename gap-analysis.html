Prototype Gap Analysis

A screen-by-screen audit of the HKHT AI for Seva prototype from the perspective of the five operating personas, plus the two areas the prototype most under-builds: multi-tenant central billing (orgs → centers → departments billed centrally for voice / WhatsApp / AI / ad APIs) and micro-level operational tracking (done vs needs-improvement, pending approval, audit, ownership, SLAs). Each gap states the current state, the gap, the operational impact, and exactly how to implement it in this codebase (js/seed.js entities, js/store.js actions, js/components.js UI, screen files under js/screens/).

Generated for the HKHT AI for Seva Phase-1 prototype (mock-only). Severity: P0 blocks real operuse · P1 high friction / trust gap · P2 meaningful polish · P3 nice-to-have. Effort: S <½ day · M 1–2 days · L 3+ days, in this prototype's vanilla-JS architecture.
