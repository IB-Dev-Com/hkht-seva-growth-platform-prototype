WF-007 is meant to become the donor relationship and revenue-cultivation module of the platform. It must help HKHT turn contacts, Yatra participants, online donors, community contacts, temple visitors, HNI prospects and CSR prospects into long-term donor relationships.

3. Intended Architecture from the Beginning
Source basis: ; Important Upgrades – Claude; KCKE–Media–CRM–ERP Dependency Map; Project Glossary; latest Document B.
The following items should be understood as part of the intended core architecture and agentic development thought process, not as late additions.
Architecture element | Correct intended understanding
Relationship intelligence | WF-007 should preserve a full donor relationship journey: contact source, campaign, call, meeting, donation, Yatra, hospitality, prasadam, family/community/referral links and future opportunities.
Predictive intelligence | Initial rules can start early; the data model must support future donor propensity, recurrence prediction, lapsed donor risk and Yatra/donor upgrade signals.
Real-time AI assistance | The near-term base should support pre-meeting donor briefs; future roadmap can support live fundraiser copilot guidance.
Institutional memory | Donor judgment, approved cultivation patterns and leadership guidance should become reusable organizational memory, not only temporary chat responses.
KCKE source-grounding | Use KCKE for source-grounded Krishna conscious donor narratives, festival meanings and preaching content. Do not use KCKE for payment, contact, task or approval status.
Media AI | Use Media AI for donor inspiration videos, Yatra recaps, festival media and approved visual storytelling. Do not treat Media AI as CRM, ERP or spiritual source of truth.
CRM/DBMS operational truth | CRM/DBMS is the relationship data backbone for contacts, donors, source, follow-ups, campaigns and touchpoints.
ERP-ready records | ERP is for later validated financial and operational records: payments, receipts, approvals, inventory and finance.
Gemini Enterprise productivity support | GE is suitable for internal drafting, summaries, search, no-code workflows and staff-assist use cases.
Vertex AI / Agent Builder path | Production-grade multi-step agents, batch processing, connectors and event orchestration may need Google Cloud/Vertex/Agent Builder beyond GE.
BigQuery / Looker intelligence | Analytics, dashboards, forecasting and leadership command center should use an analytics layer such as BigQuery/Looker where appropriate.
ClickUp task governance | ClickUp should act as task execution layer where task routing and meeting/action governance are involved.
Leadership command center | WF-007 must contribute donor revenue, pipeline, follow-up, approval and exception metrics to one leadership view.
WF-013 multi-center readiness | The design should be templated and exportable for future multi-center replication after Hyderabad stabilization.
