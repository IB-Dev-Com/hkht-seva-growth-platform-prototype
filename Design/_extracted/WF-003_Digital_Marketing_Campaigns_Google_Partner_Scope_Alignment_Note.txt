WF-003 Digital Marketing Campaigns – Google Partner Scope Alignment and Improvement Note
Partner-facing note to align technical scope with intended HKHT AI Revenue & Seva Growth Platform architecture
1. Purpose of This Note
This note is meant to help the Google Partner / implementation partner align the technical scope for WF-003 Digital Marketing Campaigns with the intended HKHT AI for Seva architecture.
This is not a rejection of the current partner work. It is not adding random new scope. It is a clarification of the intended architecture so the partner can classify, phase and strengthen the implementation plan correctly.
The key request is simple: early delivery may start small, but the design must be production-shaped, CRM-aligned, ERP-ready, dashboard-visible, human-governed and platform-compatible from Day 1.
Source basis: Latest WF-003 Document B - 30-Day Production-Ready Accelerated Execution Brief; HKHT GE Technical Scope; HKHT GE Technical Scope Gap Analysis; .
2. Core Understanding Expected from the Partner
WF-003 is the digital acquisition and campaign intelligence module of the Hyderabad AI Revenue & Seva Growth Platform. It should not be reduced to a small isolated AI-copy demo or a disconnected dashboard. It must help HKHT connect campaign planning, content, audience, spend, landing page, leads, CRM follow-up, donation, Yatra registration, donor cultivation and leadership visibility.
It should preserve common IDs such as Contact ID, Donor ID, Yatri ID, Campaign ID, Event ID, Donation ID, Payment ID and Task ID where applicable.
It should preserve source tracking: campaign source, ad source, message source, website/landing-page source, call source, Yatra source, donor source and referral source.
It should include approvals for budget, public communication, donor-sensitive messaging, HNI/CSR messaging, devotional content, media and financial decisions.
It should feed dashboards that show leads, calls, registrations, donations, conversions, delays, escalations and revenue/P&L where applicable.
It should remain CRM-centric for relationship data and ERP-ready for validated financial/operational data.
It should support future relationship intelligence, predictive intelligence and multi-center readiness by capturing clean data from Day 1.
Source basis: Latest Document B Platform Role and CRM/ERP/Data sections; Gap Analysis WF-003; Sources of Leads file; Less Investment - More ROI Campaigns file.

5. Scope Alignment with HKHT GE Technical Scope vs Gap Analysis
The HKHT GE Technical Scope correctly recognizes WF-003 as a connector-type workflow around campaign planning, content briefing and spend-to-revenue attribution. It also notes that production requires Ads/Meta/Analytics connectors, true spend-to-lead-to-donation attribution, scheduled refresh, feeds to WF-002/WF-001 and source scoring to WF-006.
The Gap Analysis clarifies that the larger need is not only campaign insight preparation. HKHT must know whether marketing money actually creates registrations, donations and long-term relationships. Therefore, partner scope should explicitly address campaign event writes to CRM, canonical attribution model, source persistence, lead linkage across workflows and a single marketing intelligence layer.

7. What the Partner Should Not Miss
Common IDs: Contact ID, Donor ID, Yatri ID, Campaign ID, Event ID, Yatra ID, Donation ID, Payment ID, Receipt ID, Task ID and Owner ID where applicable.
CRM must remain the relationship source of truth for people, source, follow-up, consent/DND, touchpoints, campaign history and donor/yatri relationship journey.
ERP/payment/DCC systems should remain the validated source for payments, receipts and financial records as integration matures.
Approval gates must block sensitive actions until a human approver signs off.
Audit logs must show who created, changed, approved, launched or escalated items.
Dashboards must show campaign spend, leads, calls, donations, Yatra registrations, conversions, pending follow-ups, escalations and revenue/P&L where applicable.
Role ownership must be validated; do not rely only on old role files or transcript-derived names.
Fallback process is required if ad/CRM/payment/vendor API data is unavailable.
API registry is required for Google Ads, Meta Ads, GA4, CRM, DCC/payment, WhatsApp/RCS/SMS, ClickUp and landing pages.
Golden journey testing must show a sample contact from source to CRM to follow-up to outcome to dashboard.
KCKE is used only for source-grounded Krishna conscious content, not operational records.
Media AI is used for creative/media/storyboards, not as CRM, ERP or source of spiritual truth.
WF-013 multi-center readiness requires reusable templates, fields, source taxonomy and playbooks, not immediate multi-center deployment.
