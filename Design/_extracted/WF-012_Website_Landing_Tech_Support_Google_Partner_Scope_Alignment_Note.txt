WF-013 multi-center readiness | Templates, IDs, QA checklists, dashboards, and role patterns should be reusable later by other centers.

KCKE and Media AI boundaries must be respected.
WF-013 multi-center readiness must be protected through reusable templates, IDs, QA, and governance.
