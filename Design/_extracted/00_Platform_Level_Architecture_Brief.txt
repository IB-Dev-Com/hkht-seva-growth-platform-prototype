Executive Summary
This document converts the latest workflow-wise implementation briefs into one practical governance artifact for the Hyderabad AI Revenue & Seva Growth Platform. It is designed to help HKHT leadership, workflow owners and the implementation partner coordinate accelerated delivery without creating isolated automations.
The package follows a 30-Day Production Base + 60-Day Stabilization + 75-Day Enterprise Scale operating model. The first 30 days focus on production base and initial live use; the 60-day phase stabilizes the system; the 75-day phase establishes enterprise-scale readiness.
Platform Vision
The platform must combine revenue generation, seva expansion, preaching reach, operational control, donor/yatri relationship intelligence, and managerial leverage. Each workflow remains a specific module but shares identity, source tracking, approvals, dashboards and integration discipline.

AI-First Operating Model
Treat current process evidence as the problem record, not the target design.
Use deterministic automation for validation, reminders, routing, status changes and dashboard updates.
Use AI assistance for classification, summarization, drafting, recommendation, anomaly detection and next-best-action support.
Use agentic workflows only after data quality, approval routing and fallback paths are validated.
Keep sensitive decisions under human approval while using AI to prepare decision-quality context.
Core Architecture Layers
Layer | Purpose | Primary Workflows | Boundary Rule
CRM / DBMS relationship backbone | Single operational relationship layer for contacts, donors, yatris, leads, community residents and touchpoints. | WF-001, WF-002, WF-003, WF-006, WF-007, WF-014, WF-012 | CRM stores operational relationship facts, not scripture or media storyboards.
ERP-ready operating structure | Future structured financial and operational system of record with ERP-ready IDs and fields from Day 1. | WF-009, WF-010, WF-011 and finance/payment dependencies across revenue workflows | Do not delay useful pilots, but design every entity for later ERP mapping.
Integration layer | Connects forms, sheets, CRM, payment systems, WhatsApp, voice, websites, ads, ClickUp and dashboards. | All workflows | Every integration must have owner, access status, fallback and test case.
Analytics layer | Shared KPI, attribution and exception reporting layer. | All workflows | Dashboards must roll up to the leadership command center.
KCKE boundary | Source-grounded Krishna conscious knowledge and devotional reference layer. | WF-004, WF-005, WF-007, WF-012, WF-014 | Do not store operational CRM/payment/task status in KCKE.
Media AI Pipeline boundary | Scene-level media creation, storyboard, asset selection, approval and publishing support. | WF-004, WF-010, WF-012, WF-014 and content-related donor/Yatra flows | Do not treat generated media as approved without human review.
Gemini Enterprise / productivity agents | Workspace productivity, meeting summaries, document search, task support, internal copilots. | WF-008, WF-011 and cross-workflow support | Do not confuse productivity agents with KCKE or Media AI.
ClickUp execution layer | Task ownership, due dates, follow-up visibility, escalation and meeting execution. | WF-008 and workflows with task handoff | ClickUp remains task execution layer; AI prepares and routes but does not replace accountability.

Human Approval Governance
Human approval is mandatory for donor-sensitive messages, finance/payment changes, public communications, devotional/philosophical content, deity/media representation, HR/procurement decisions and exceptional situations.
Every workflow must validate owner, performer, approver, backup and escalation path before production routing.
Approvals must be traceable through task IDs, review status and decision log entries where applicable.
Assumptions / Needs Validation
Final production access status for several APIs is Assumption / Needs Validation until confirmed by the partner and HKHT owners.
WF-013 is generated as a blueprint in this package based on current workflows and must be validated by leadership.
The leadership command center can begin with Looker Studio or equivalent dashboarding before later platform maturity.
Immediate Next Actions
Approve the platform architecture brief as the binding constraint for all individual workflow builds.
Assign a platform integration owner and data model owner.
Use the Master Entity Model and API Registry before allowing new workflow-specific tables or connectors.
Require every workflow team to map its dashboard KPIs to the leadership command center.
