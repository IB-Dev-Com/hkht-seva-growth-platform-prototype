Workforce Planning & Requisition Agent
Purpose: Converts role needs into structured requisitions, JD drafts, role scorecards and approval packets.
Users: Mukund Prabhu, HR Manager, HODs.
Inputs: Role need, department, urgency, vacancy count, CTC range, skills, experience, revenue-critical flag.
Outputs: Approved/ready-to-approve requisition, JD, interview matrix, missing-info list.
Data needed: Employee/role master, department map, prior JD templates, approval matrix.
Tools/connectors: Google Forms/AppSheet/Sheets, Docs, Gmail, Gemini, later ERP/ATS.
Human approval points: HR Manager/HOD/Mukund approval for role, CTC and key hires.
Escalation logic: Escalate if P0/P1 revenue role remains unapproved >48 hours or critical field missing.
Success metrics: Time to approved JD, requisition completeness, revenue-critical vacancy days.
Evidence basis: [Sources: S2, S6, S8, S9]
Recruitment Screening Agent
Purpose: Screens candidates against role scorecards and summarizes fit/gaps.
Users: HR Executive, HR Manager, interviewer panel.
Inputs: CVs, job portal exports, JD, scorecard, role criteria.
Outputs: Candidate shortlist, fit score, interview questions, risk notes.
Data needed: Candidate source, resume, experience, skills, current CTC/expected CTC if available.
Tools/connectors: Drive, Sheets, Gmail, Gemini/Vertex, ATS/job portal exports.
Human approval points: Human shortlist/interview decision; no autonomous rejection for sensitive cases without HR review.
Escalation logic: Escalate low-fit pipeline, insufficient candidates, CTC mismatch, or duplicate applicant.
Success metrics: Time-to-shortlist, shortlist quality, interview conversion.
Evidence basis: [Sources: S2, S6, S8, S9]
Interview & Offer Governance Agent
Purpose: Ensures interview rounds, scorecards, fixed/variable CTC, approvals and offer readiness are complete.
Users: HR Manager, Finance, SME interviewers.
Inputs: Interview scores, comments, CTC proposal, approval limits.
Outputs: Offer readiness packet, missing approval alerts, fixed/variable split validation.
Data needed: Candidate ID, requisition ID, scorecard, offer template, approver list.
Tools/connectors: Sheets/AppSheet, Docs, Gmail, HRMS/ERP later.
Human approval points: Finance/HR/hiring approver approval; CTC deviations require leadership approval.
Escalation logic: Escalate missing fixed/variable pay, missing score, unapproved CTC, or payroll/legal risk.
Success metrics: Offer error rate, approval cycle time, rework count.
Evidence basis: [Sources: S2, S6, S8, S9]
Onboarding & Access Agent
Purpose: Creates and tracks day-1 readiness: employee ID, workspace, computer, email, access, training and KT.
Users: HR Executive, Admin, IT, manager, new employee.
Inputs: Offer acceptance, role, start date, location, access needs, equipment needs.
Outputs: Onboarding checklist, task list, access/equipment completion, training plan.
Data needed: Employee profile, role map, equipment/access list, SOP/KT modules.
